Types of Snake


There are 5 Primary snake species that are currently popular
with beginners, those species are:

  • Corn Snake, (Pantherophis guttata)
  • California Kingsnake, (Lampropeltis getula californiae)
  • Rosy Boa, (Lichanura trivirgata)
  • Gopher Snake, (Pituophis spp)
  • Ball Python, (Python regius)

Snake Enclosure

With the Enclosures avaiable you can't go wrong,
most pet stores have standart reptile tanks available
and can help you with the creation of your snake habitat
there are certain snakes that need a higher humidity,
certain snakes that are shy from nature that need
more hiding spots and certain snakes that like to bath.

Snake Diet

The snake it's diet is based on the type of snake you choose,
if you choose one of the snakes listed above,
they can live on small mice that can be bought in the pet store
under the catagory "feeding mice"

there are also other type of snakes that eat allot of different things,
like the thread snake that eats pupae, eggs or ants.
there are also bigger snakes like pythons and anacondas
that have been known to eat deer and pigs.

Snake Behaviour

The snake his/her behaviour is different with every snake,
some snakes want to go outside with you and will stay on your arm,
but others might want to stay inside the house
and not be touched without them allowing it.

With certain species comes certain behaviours, One might like to stay hidden and needs extra shelter places to hide in
